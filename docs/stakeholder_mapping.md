# Hotel guests [HIGHLY AFFECTED, low power]

## What matters most to me?

Easy, predictable control and comfortable lighting.

## What keeps me up at night?

I can't figure out how to control the lights.

## What is the key question I need an answer to?

Will I understand this in 5 seconds without instructions?

## What do we need from them?

To use the system and give feedback.

# Hotel maintenance staff [HIGHLY AFFECTED, low power]

## What matters most to me?

A Fast and effortless way to turn on maintenance mode for full lighting to clean the room.

## What keeps me up at night?

Wasting due to issues with turning dim lighting scenes into full brightness to do my work efficiently.

## What is the key question I need an answer to?

How do I reset the controller fast if it fails to read the staff card for maintenance mode?

## What do we need from them?

Feedback on any issues when using staff-only modes.

# System installer partner [not affected, low power]

# Lighting supplier partner [not affected, HIGH POWER]

## What matters most to me?

Smooth delivery and compatibility, clear responsibilities and avoiding blame and additional costs from any issues.

## What keeps me up at night?

Issues with lights that force us to re-manufacture and ship big batches of material and pay for additional installation work. Many failures during the warranty period.

## What is the key question I need an answer to?

Exactly which compatibility requirements must we meet, and who is responsible for what if any issues occur.

## What do we need from them?

To provide compatible, high-quality lights that meet our specifications.
To take responsibility for incompatibilities and defects during the warranty period.
